## List of Symbols (Discrete Mathematics)  
CS-113 Discrete Structures

#### Logic
- _p_ ∨ _q_: _p_ or _q_
- _p_ ∧ _q_: _p_ and _q_
- ¬ _p_, -_p_, _p_ bar: not _p_
- _p_ ⇒ _q_: if _p_, then _q_
- _p_ ⇔ _q_: _p_ if and only if _q_
- _P_ ≡ _Q_: _P_ and _Q_ are logically equivalent
- ∀: for all
- ∃: there exists
- ∴: therefore
